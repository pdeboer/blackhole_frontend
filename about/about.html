What is it all about?

Here at the University of Zurich in company with the ETH of Zurich we are trying to locate new, not yet inspected blackholes. For this purpose we need a lot of helping hands which are great at seeing special features on several pictures.


Here are some key figures about the current study:

  • Over 48'000 potential blackholes
  • With 8 different scales almost 2mio Pictures from 3 datasources
  • Over 50'000 unique spectras
